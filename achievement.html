Fateen's Achievement

PM Narendra Modi Congratulating fateen for winning Rashtriya Bal Puraskar

Fateen was conferred a National award, “ The Prestigious Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Bal Puraskar” for his outstanding achievements representing India in the field of Arts & Culture and bringing laurels to the Nation. The award ceremony held annually on Republic Day at the Rashtrapati Bhavan was held virtually due to covid crisis on 24th January 2022 and he was awarded by the honorable Prime Minister.
